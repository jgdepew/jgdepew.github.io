About Me

Born in California, I moved to Seattle to attend the University of Washington. Up until then, I thought I knew what career I wanted to pursue. But like many other students, I quickly realized that path wasn't for me. It took a while to find a career that captivated my attention and challenged the way I thought. After graduating from UW, I pursued my passion of Web Development and enrolled in Coding Dojo.

Web Development isn't my only passion. I played a multitude of sports growing up but am a soccer player first and foremost. I support our local Seattle Sounders as well as Chelsea FC. In addition, you can almost always find me plugged into headphones jamming away to the latest and greatest tunes or listening for how I can improve some songs I've written myself.

Go Fish

A program that will run a simulation of "Go Fish" between 4 computer players. Each computer randomly picks a card from their hand and randomly picks an opponent from which to request that random card value. The game is finished once a player runs out of cards and the winner is decided by the player with the score. Each player gains a point when they gain a pair of matching card values.

Technical skills used:

  • Python
  • OOP
  • Git/Github
