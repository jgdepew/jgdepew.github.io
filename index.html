About Me

Born in California, I moved to Seattle to attend the University of Washington. Up until then, I thought I knew what I wanted to become professionally. But like many other students, I quickly realized the profession wasn't for me. It took a while to find a career that captivated my attention and challenged the way I thought. After graduating from UW, I pursued my passion of Web Development and enrolled in Coding Dojo.

Web Development isn't my only passion. I played a multitude of sports growing up but was a soccer player first and foremost. I support our local Seattle Sounders as well as Chelsea FC. In addition, you can almost always find me plugged into headphones jamming away to the latest and greatest tunes or listening for how I can improve some songs I've written myself.
